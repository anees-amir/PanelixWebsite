COLLISION & STRUCTURAL

Core Services

PANEL BEATING

From minor door dings to severe collision damage, our panel beaters restore your vehicle's bodywork to exact factory specification using a combination of traditional hammer-and-dolly techniques and modern hydraulic straightening equipment.

  • Full factory-spec panel restoration
  • Hydraulic pulling and straightening systems
  • Traditional hammer, dolly and shrinking techniques
  • Pre and post-repair measurements verified
  • All vehicles inspected for hidden structural damage

SMASH REPAIRS

Comprehensive accident repair covering both structural and cosmetic damage. We work directly with all major Australian insurers and manage the entire claims process on your behalf — from assessment to completion.

  • End-to-end insurance claims management
  • Direct insurer billing — no upfront costs
  • Structural integrity assessment included
  • Air-bag and sensor recalibration post-repair
  • Full photographic documentation for your records

PRECISION PANEL & STRUCTURAL ALIGNMENT

Using computerised 3D measuring systems, we restore your vehicle's chassis and panel alignment to within OEM tolerances. Correct alignment is critical for ride, handling, tyre wear — and most importantly, safety.

  • 3D computerised chassis measurement
  • Bench pulling to OEM specification
  • Uni-body and ladder-frame vehicle support
  • Post-repair measurement report provided
  • Wheel alignment check included
02

PAINT & FINISH

Refinishing

SPRAY PAINTING & OEM COLOUR MATCHING

Our computerised spectrophotometer reads your vehicle's exact paint code and mixes a perfect match — accounting for fade, metallic flake and pearl pigment. From single panels to full resprays, the result is invisible.

  • Computerised OEM colour matching system
  • Waterborne and solvent-borne paint options
  • Climate-controlled spray booths
  • Spot, panel and full-vehicle resprays
  • Ceramic and clear-coat protection available

DENT & HAIL RESTORATION

Paintless dent repair (PDR) for hail damage, door dings and low-impact dents — no filler, no repainting. We use precision rods and LED lighting to massage the metal back to its original form without disturbing the factory finish.

  • True PDR — no filler, no paint
  • LED lighting rigs for precise dent mapping
  • Hail damage assessments & reports
  • Insurance-approved PDR methodology
  • Same-day turnaround available for minor damage

BODYKIT & FACELIFTS

Transform your vehicle's look with aftermarket bodykits, custom bumpers, side skirts, spoilers and cosmetic upgrades — all professionally fitted and colour-matched to your factory or custom finish. From subtle refinements to full aesthetic overhauls.

  • OEM and aftermarket bodykit fitting
  • Custom bumper fabrication and replacement
  • Side skirts, spoilers and diffuser installation
  • Full colour-match to factory or custom codes
  • Cosmetic facelift packages available

RUST TREATMENT & PREVENTION

Rust spreads fast once it takes hold. We identify, cut back and treat corrosion at the source, then seal and protect the area before repainting to match. Protect your vehicle's structural integrity and resale value.

  • Full rust assessment and mapping
  • Chemical conversion treatment
  • Panel fabrication where cutting is required
  • Cavity wax and underbody protection
  • Post-treatment paint matched and sealed

Our Story

BUILT ON
INTEGRITY

For over 20 years, Panelix has been Melbourne's go-to panel beating shop. What started as a one-man workshop has grown into a 8-strong team of certified technicians who care about one thing: returning your car to you in better condition than you left it.

We use cutting-edge frame straightening equipment, waterborne eco-paints, and computerised colour matching — all backed by a lifetime workmanship guarantee on every repair.

  • Certified by all major insurers
  • Lifetime workmanship warranty
  • Free loan cars available
  • Eco-friendly waterborne paints
